Newspapers and and its layout
==============================

The Spiritualist contains three types of newspapers under the title.

* The Spiritualist
* Spiritualist The Newspaper
* Psyche

The different issues and their respective paper ids can found in the CSV file `here <../../spiritualist_issue_data.csv>`_.

The newspaper can contain different types of information for example,

1. Articles
2. Advertisements
3. Drawings or sketches

Also the three newspapers in The Spiritualist have different page layouts.
